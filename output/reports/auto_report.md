## 12. Methodology

**Data Collection:** 7 platforms scraped — Reddit (public JSON API), YouTube (native search + page scraping), TikTok/Twitter/Instagram/LinkedIn/Threads (Apify cloud actors, API tokens required).

**Text Processing:** English-only filtering via langdetect (min 20 chars). Deduplication by first 200 characters of normalized text. Date standardization to YYYY-MM-DD.

**Sentiment:** VADER lexicon-based analysis. Thresholds: positive ≥ 0.05, negative ≤ -0.05, neutral in between.

**Engagement Normalization:** Z-score per platform (engagement - platform_mean) / platform_std. Enables fair cross-platform comparison independent of audience size.

**Anomaly Detection Thresholds:**
- Engagement spikes: > rolling 7-day mean + 3σ AND ≥ 3x day-over-day increase
- Mention spikes: > rolling 7-day mean + 2.5σ
- Viral posts: > 50× platform median engagement
- Keyword trends: ≥ 2× frequency increase (older vs newer data half)

**Statistical Tests:** Kruskal-Wallis H-test for multi-group comparison, Mann-Whitney U for two-group comparison, Chi-squared for categorical distributions, Spearman correlation for continuous relationships. Significance level: α = 0.05.

**Topic Discovery:** TF-IDF vectorization (max 200 features, min_df=3, max_df=0.8). Emerging terms detected by comparing word frequencies in chronologically split halves (≥ 1.5× growth).
